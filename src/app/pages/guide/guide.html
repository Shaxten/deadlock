Mechanics Guide

All key timers, objectives, and mechanics you need to know to dominate in Deadlock.

Map Timers & Objectives

📦

Crates / Boxes

First Spawn 2:00
Respawn 3:00 after broken
Mid Boxes 10:00
Drop Chance 60%
Base Value 23 souls (+2/min)

518 crates on the map. Souls are unsecured — collect them quickly or they delay respawn. At 10 min a crate is worth ~43 souls, at 20 min ~63 souls.

Source: deadlock.wiki
🗿

Golden Statues

First Spawn 2:00
Respawn 3:00 after broken
Drop Chance 50%
Mid Statues 8 extra at 10:00

Drops a permanent stat buff (random): Fire Rate, Max Ammo, Cooldown, Weapon Damage, Max Health, or Spirit Power. Buffs scale in 3 levels: Lv1 (0 min), Lv2 (10 min), Lv3 (30 min).

Source: deadlock.wiki
🎰

Sinner's Sacrifice

First Spawn 8:00
Respawn 5:00 after cleared
Locations 10 sites
Base Bounty 310 souls (+3.35/min)
Jackpot Reward 4 Golden Statue buffs

Melee-only (500 HP). Deals 80 damage back per hit. Hit the jackpot timing window (last 1.3s when all bulbs lit) with a heavy melee for 4 permanent buffs. Light melee jackpot gives 1 buff.

Source: deadlock.wiki

Small Camp Spawn 2:00 (respawn 1:25)
Medium Camp Spawn 6:00 (respawn 4:50)

Type Souls Growth HP

Small camps have 3 denizens (123 total souls). Large denizens take +20% melee damage. All denizens have 50% bullet resist and 45% spirit resist (large: 60%/55%). Souls split between all players who damaged them.

Soul Urn

First Spawn 10:00
Respawn Every 5:00
First Lane Yellow Lane

Alternates lanes each spawn. Delivering the urn grants a large soul payout to your team. Carrier is visible on the map and can be killed to drop it. First spawn is always on Yellow Lane.

Source: deadlock.wiki

Mid Boss (Temple Guardian)

First Spawn 10:00
Respawn 4:00 after killed
Location Center pit (underground)

Drops the Rejuvenator crystal. Requires multiple players. A global roar announces its spawn. Cannot be damaged from range — you must be close.

Source: deadlock.wiki
💎

Rejuvenator

Buff Duration 4:00 (240s)
Respawn Reduction -50% / -60% / -70%
Trooper Buff +70% / +100% / +130% HP

Punch the crystal 3 times to shatter it. Grants team-wide buff: +15% Health, +15% Fire Rate, +15% Spirit Damage, plus shared Rejuvenator credits. Scales with each subsequent kill.

🤖

Walkers (Guardians)

Tier 1 Lane guardians (outer)
Tier 2 Inner guardians
Bullet Resist 25%

Walkers protect lanes and must be destroyed to push. They deal heavy damage to nearby heroes. Backdoor protection gives them extra bullet resistance when no friendly creeps are nearby.

⛩️

Shrines

First Shrine HP 5,000
Second Shrine HP 10,000
On Death Zipline-boosted waves

Located in each base. Destroying a shrine weakens the enemy's defenses and triggers boosted trooper waves. Second shrine is much tankier.

👑

Patron (Base Core)

Condition Shrines must be down
Phases 2 phases
Win Condition Destroy to win

The final objective. Can only be damaged after both shrines are destroyed. Has two health phases with increasing resistance.

Core Mechanics

🎯 Denying

Shoot enemy soul orbs (orange) before they light up to deny them. The enemy loses those souls — you don't gain them. Ally orbs are green. Orbs can be claimed with a single bullet regardless of damage.

🔒 Securing

When a trooper dies, 60% drops as a ground orb (auto-collected by nearby allies, cannot be denied) and 40% as a floating deniable orb. Killing with melee gives 100% instantly with no orb spawned.

💀 Soul Sharing

Trooper souls split among allies within 45m of the orb or 30m of the killer. Rates: 100% / 54% / 36% / 25% / 20% / 16% for 1–6 players. Don't stack lanes — you all earn less.

⚡ Parry

Heavy melee (hold melee) can be parried by the target. Successful parry stuns the attacker for 2s. Guardians can also be parried — they get stunned and can't melee for 5s after.

🏃 Stamina

Used for dashing, double jumping, and climbing. Regenerates over time. Manage it carefully in fights — no stamina means no escape or repositioning.

📈 Boons (Level Ups)

First boon at 600 souls, then 900, 1200, 1500, 2100, 2800, 3600… up to 40,600 (max level 35). Each boon increases bullet damage, melee damage, spirit power, and health. Ability unlocks at levels 0, 2, 4, 6.

🔓 Unsecured Souls

Souls from crates, denizens, and Sinner's Sacrifice are unsecured. They're lost on death if you have 150+, dropping a Soul Container. They convert to secured over time (0.5%/s + 1.6 base/s).

🛡️ Bullet vs Spirit Armor

Two separate damage types. Build armor items based on what the enemy team deals most. Check their heroes and items. Walkers have 25% bullet resist but 0% spirit resist.

Game Phases

0:00 – 10:00

Early Game (Laning)

Focus on last-hitting, denying, and trading with your lane opponent. Break crates on cooldown. Take neutral camps between waves. Win your lane to get item advantage.

10:00 – 20:00

Mid Game (Rotations)

Soul Urn and Mid Boss become active. Start rotating for objectives. Group for urn deliveries. Contest or take Mid Boss. Push walkers when you have numbers advantage.

20:00+

Late Game (Teamfights)

Full 6v6 fights decide the game. Rejuvenator control is critical. Push shrines after winning fights. Death timers are long — one wipe can end the game.
