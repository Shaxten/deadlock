Game Knowledge

Mechanics Guide

All key timers, objectives, and mechanics you need to know to dominate in Deadlock.

Map Timers & Objectives

📦

Crates / Boxes

First Spawn 2:00
Respawn 3:00 after broken
Mid Boxes 10:00

Souls from crates are unsecured. Break them regularly for extra income. Mid-area boxes above the pit spawn later at 10 min.

🗿

Golden Statues

First Spawn 3:00
Respawn 3:00 after broken
Reward ~200 souls

Located near lanes. Higher value than regular crates. Prioritize these when rotating.

👹

Neutral Camps

First Spawn 1:00
Respawn 4:00
Reward ~135 souls/camp

Great filler income between waves. Don't miss lane creeps for camps early game.

⚱️

Soul Urn

First Spawn 10:00
Respawn Every 5:00
First Lane Yellow Lane

Alternates lanes each spawn. Delivering the urn grants a large soul payout to your team. Carrier is visible on the map and can be killed to drop it.

🐉

Mid Boss (Temple Guardian)

First Spawn 10:00
Respawn 4:00 after killed
Location Center pit (underground)

Drops the Rejuvenator crystal. Requires multiple players. A global roar announces its spawn. Cannot be damaged from range — you must be close.

💎

Rejuvenator

Buff Duration 4:00 (240s)
Respawn Reduction -50% / -60% / -70%
Trooper Buff +70% / +100% / +130% HP

Punch the crystal 3 times to shatter it. Grants team-wide buff: +15% Health, +15% Fire Rate, +15% Spirit Damage, plus shared Rejuvenator credits. Scales with each subsequent kill.

🤖

Walkers (Guardians)

Tier 1 Lane guardians (outer)
Tier 2 Inner guardians
Bullet Resist 25%

Walkers protect lanes and must be destroyed to push. They deal heavy damage to nearby heroes. Backdoor protection gives them extra bullet resistance when no friendly creeps are nearby.

⛩️

Shrines

First Shrine HP 5,000
Second Shrine HP 10,000
On Death Zipline-boosted waves

Located in each base. Destroying a shrine weakens the enemy's defenses and triggers boosted trooper waves. Second shrine is much tankier.

👑

Patron (Base Core)

Condition Shrines must be down
Phases 2 phases
Win Condition Destroy to win

The final objective. Can only be damaged after both shrines are destroyed. Has two health phases with increasing resistance.

Core Mechanics

🎯 Denying

Shoot enemy soul orbs (yellow) to deny them. You get a portion of the denied souls. Essential for winning lane.

🔒 Securing

Last-hit your own creeps' soul orbs (green) to secure them. Unsecured orbs can be denied by enemies.

💀 Soul Sharing

Before 10 min: nearby players get 100% soul value. After 10 min: souls split among nearby players. Don't stack lanes late.

⚡ Parry

Heavy melee (hold melee) can be parried. Successful parry stuns the attacker. Timing is tight but game-changing in duels.

🏃 Stamina

Used for dashing, double jumping, and climbing. Regenerates over time. Manage it carefully in fights — no stamina means no escape.

📈 Boons (Level Ups)

Earned at soul thresholds (starting at 13.2k, then every 1.5k). Each boon gives stat upgrades. Reach 40k for all boons.

🔫 Headshots

Deal bonus damage. Crit multiplier varies by hero and range. Some items boost headshot damage significantly.

🛡️ Bullet vs Spirit Armor

Two separate damage types. Build armor items based on what the enemy team deals most. Check their heroes and items.

Game Phases

0:00 – 10:00

Early Game (Laning)

Focus on last-hitting, denying, and trading with your lane opponent. Break crates on cooldown. Take neutral camps between waves. Win your lane to get item advantage.

10:00 – 20:00

Mid Game (Rotations)

Soul Urn and Mid Boss become active. Start rotating for objectives. Group for urn deliveries. Contest or take Mid Boss. Push walkers when you have numbers advantage.

20:00+

Late Game (Teamfights)

Full 6v6 fights decide the game. Rejuvenator control is critical. Push shrines after winning fights. Death timers are long — one wipe can end the game.
