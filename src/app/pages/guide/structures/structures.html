Game Knowledge

Structures & Units

Everything you need to know about troopers, guardians, walkers, shrines, and objectives in Deadlock.

Troopers

🔫

Ranged Trooper

Health 300 HP
DPS 28
Range 25m

Standard ranged attacker. Forms the backbone of every wave.

⚔️

Melee Trooper

Health 400 HP
Melee Damage 80
Move Speed 8.1 m/s

Tanky frontline unit that charges into melee range. Added to waves after 5 minutes.

💊

Medic Trooper

Health 350 HP
DPS 28
Heal Pack 12% missing HP + 150 + 2/min
Heals Troopers? ✅ Yes (small amount)

Drops a heal pack on death in a 35m radius. When a player picks it up, it heals nearby heroes AND gives a small heal to nearby allied troopers. The pack cannot be picked up by the Medic's own team. Heal scales with game time.

Source: deadlock.wiki
📋

Wave Composition & Timing

Before 5 min 3 Ranged + 1 Medic
After 5 min 2 Ranged + 1 Melee + 1 Medic
Spawn (0–20 min) Every 30s
Spawn (20–35 min) Every 25s
Spawn (35+ min) Every 20s

Waves get faster as the game progresses, increasing lane pressure over time.

💰

Soul Bounty

Base Bounty 116 souls
Growth +1.16/min per trooper
Melee kill 100% on kill (no orb)
Ranged kill 60% ground orb + 40% floating

Killing with melee gives 100% souls instantly. Ranged kills drop a ground orb (60%) and a floating orb (40%) that can be confirmed or denied. Souls split among nearby allies.

Source: deadlock.wiki
💪

Super Troopers

Damage Bonus +60%
Health Bonus +40%
Trigger Enemy shrine destroyed

Activated when an enemy shrine is destroyed. Blue lane gets Super Troopers when either shrine falls. Significantly increases push power.

Guardians

🛡️

Lane Guardian

Health 5,500 HP
Soul Bounty 1,500 souls (team-wide)
DPS 116 (laser)
Bullet Resistance 10%
Damage Resist 75% → -50% over 12 min

Immune when no enemies within 24m. Starts with 75% damage resist that decays to -50% by 12 min — much easier to kill late game. Can be parried when it melees (stuns it for 2s). Bounty split among all alive allies.

Source: deadlock.wiki
🏰

Base Guardian

Health 4,000 HP
Soul Bounty 1,000 souls (team-wide)
Bullet Resist (solo) 40%
Bullet Resist (6 heroes) -80%
On Kill Zipline speed boost

Bullet resist drops by 20% per nearby enemy hero — bring the whole team. Killing both base guardians in a lane grants a permanent zipline speed boost. Regenerates 65 HP/s during backdoor protection.

Source: deadlock.wiki

Walkers

🤖

Walker

HP (1st Walker) 6,000 HP
HP (2 remaining) 9,000 HP
HP (last Walker) 12,000 HP
Soul Bounty 4,000 souls
Bounty Split 40% nearby / 60% whole team
Bullet Resistance 25%
Damage Resist 65% → -65% over 18 min
On Kill +1 Extra Item Slot (team)

Walkers get stronger as others die. Attacks: Fire Beam (2% max HP/s), Stomp (350 + 15% max HP, 2s stun in 15m), Fireballs (6 projectiles, 200 dmg each). Provides nearby allies +15% Bullet and Spirit Resist. Backdoor protection: 65% damage reduction + 65 HP/s regen.

Source: deadlock.wiki

Shrines

⛩️

First Shrine

Health 5,000 HP
Soul Bounty 2,000 souls
Bullet Resist (solo) 60%
Bullet Resist (6 heroes) -60%
On Destroy Super Troopers in that lane

Bullet resist drops 20% per nearby enemy hero — attack as a team. Immune to damage from more than 11m away. Patron defends it with a beam that curses nearby heroes.

Source: deadlock.wiki
🏯

Second Shrine

Health 10,000 HP
Soul Bounty 2,000 souls
Bullet Resist (solo) 60%
On Destroy Patron becomes vulnerable

HP updates to 10,000 after the first shrine dies. Both shrines must be destroyed before the Patron can be damaged. Blue lane gets Super Troopers when either shrine falls.

Source: deadlock.wiki

Patron

👑

Patron (Base Core)

Win Condition Destroy to win
Health Phases 2 phases
Prerequisite Both shrines destroyed
Shield Regenerating

The final objective. Can only be damaged after both shrines are destroyed. Has 2 health phases with a regenerating shield. Commit fully when pushing — partial damage gets healed back.

Mid-Boss (Temple Guardian)

🐉

Mid-Boss

First Spawn 10:00
1st Respawn 7 min after killed
2nd Respawn 6 min after killed
3rd+ Respawn 5 min after killed
Location Center pit
Min Players 2–3 recommended

Drops the Rejuvenator crystal. Cannot be damaged from range — you must be in melee distance. Requires 2–3 players minimum. A global roar announces its spawn. Respawn timer decreases with each kill.

💎

Rejuvenator (Drop)

Activation Punch crystal 3 times
Buff Duration 4:00 (240s)
Respawn Reduction -50% / -60% / -70%
Trooper HP Buff +70% / +100% / +130%

Grants team-wide buff: +15% Health, +15% Fire Rate, +15% Spirit Damage, plus shared Rejuvenator credits. Buff scales with each subsequent Mid-Boss kill.
