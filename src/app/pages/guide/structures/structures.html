Game Knowledge

Structures & Units

Everything you need to know about troopers, guardians, walkers, shrines, and objectives in Deadlock.

Troopers

🔫

Ranged Trooper

Health 300 HP
DPS 28
Range 25m

Standard ranged attacker. Forms the backbone of every wave.

⚔️

Melee Trooper

Health 400 HP
Melee Damage 80
Move Speed 8.1 m/s

Tanky frontline unit that charges into melee range. Added to waves after 5 minutes.

💊

Medic Trooper

Health 350 HP
DPS 28
Heal Pack 12% missing HP + 150 + 2/min

Drops a heal pack on death. Heal scales with game time. Present in all waves from the start.

📋

Wave Composition & Timing

Before 5 min 3 Ranged + 1 Medic
After 5 min 2 Ranged + 1 Melee + 1 Medic
Spawn (0–20 min) Every 30s
Spawn (20–35 min) Every 25s
Spawn (35+ min) Every 20s

Waves get faster as the game progresses, increasing lane pressure over time.

💰

Soul Bounty

Base Bounty 116 souls
Growth +1.16/min per trooper

Trooper soul value increases over time. Securing last hits is critical for economy.

💪

Super Troopers

Damage Bonus +60%
Health Bonus +40%
Trigger Enemy shrine destroyed

Activated when an enemy shrine is destroyed. Blue lane gets Super Troopers when either shrine falls. Significantly increases push power.

Guardians

🛡️

Lane Guardian

Role Protects lane path
Bullet Resistance 10%
Backdoor Protection Extra resist without creeps

Must be destroyed to advance through the lane. Has backdoor protection that grants extra resistance when no friendly creeps are nearby. Focus them when your wave is pushing.

Walkers

🤖

Tier 1 Walker (Outer)

Position Outer lane
Bullet Resistance 25%
Backdoor Protection Yes

First line of defense in each lane. Must be destroyed before pushing deeper. Deals heavy damage to heroes in range.

🦾

Tier 2 Walker (Inner)

Position Inner lane
Bullet Resistance 25%
Backdoor Protection Yes

Stronger than Tier 1. Guards the path to shrines. Backdoor protection activates when no friendly creeps are nearby, making solo pushes very difficult.

Shrines

⛩️

First Shrine

Health 5,000 HP
Bullet Resistance 10%
On Destroy Triggers Super Troopers

Destroying triggers Super Troopers in that lane. Blue lane gets Super Troopers when either shrine falls.

🏯

Second Shrine

Health 10,000 HP
Bullet Resistance 10%
On Destroy Unlocks Patron

Much tankier than the first shrine. Both shrines must be destroyed before the Patron can be damaged. Coordinate team pushes to take it down.

Patron

👑

Patron (Base Core)

Win Condition Destroy to win
Health Phases 2 phases
Prerequisite Both shrines destroyed
Shield Regenerating

The final objective. Can only be damaged after both shrines are destroyed. Has 2 health phases with a regenerating shield. Commit fully when pushing — partial damage gets healed back.

Mid-Boss (Temple Guardian)

🐉

Mid-Boss

First Spawn 10:00
1st Respawn 7 min after killed
2nd Respawn 6 min after killed
3rd+ Respawn 5 min after killed
Location Center pit
Min Players 2–3 recommended

Drops the Rejuvenator crystal. Cannot be damaged from range — you must be in melee distance. Requires 2–3 players minimum. A global roar announces its spawn. Respawn timer decreases with each kill.

💎

Rejuvenator (Drop)

Activation Punch crystal 3 times
Buff Duration 4:00 (240s)
Respawn Reduction -50% / -60% / -70%
Trooper HP Buff +70% / +100% / +130%

Grants team-wide buff: +15% Health, +15% Fire Rate, +15% Spirit Damage, plus shared Rejuvenator credits. Buff scales with each subsequent Mid-Boss kill.
